# Former dbB Members

This list was compiled from Wayback Machine captures of `dbb.at`. Static roster pages were treated as member evidence. Later Clansphere forum/profile pages were also checked because the site no longer exposed a clean roster there; names from that era are listed separately with their evidence type.

The later Clansphere `index.php?mod=users` list was not treated as a roster because some archived captures are generic account lists with tens of thousands of obvious spam accounts.

The source pages use inconsistent casing and spacing. Names below preserve the clearest roster form, all normalized with the `dbB+` prefix, and note obvious aliases where helpful.

## Unresolved Mentions

The July 2001 CTF member page says "and some New in trainmode" after listing Bucho, Cerberus, Iceman, Nail, and Gulak. No names are given there, so those unnamed trainees are not included in the member table.

`master` was searched for across the captured CMS-era HTML. The only clear archive hit found during this pass was prose about Nail fixing the forum, phrased as "great bow for the master," not a nickname or profile row. Until a profile, post author row, or roster entry is found, `master` remains unverified.
